AI Programmer

Job Details

Reference Number: AP103

Salary: $103k per annum

Reporting Line:Lead Programmer

Short description

We are seeking a talented AI programmer to join our technical team! As an AI programmer, you will be developing a highly advanced AI behaviours and systems that will your team in developing a believable open world. This role will require you to have strong understanding in AI algorithms, excellent communication and high proficiency in programming.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Collaborating with designers, artists and developers into creating a smart and efficient AI systems that supports them in its application and tuning.
  2. Maintaining and Improving core AI systems that define AI behaviour in an open world environment.
  3. Writing high quality code engine base that architects and enhances AI characters while aligning with the project goals
  4. Ensuring the high-quality user experience for all platforms

Requirements

  • A bachelor's degree in computer science is preferred
  • At least +5 years of professional experience as a programmer in game development, with a focus on AI systems and development.
  • Excellent understanding in AI algorithms, data structures and 3D mathematics.
  • Strong proficiency in C++ programming is essential.
  • Extensive knowledge in Unreal Engine is preferred with an understanding in ECS or Mass Framework.
  • Excellent communication skills and critical thinking skills.
  • Shipped at least 1 AAA title is preferred.
